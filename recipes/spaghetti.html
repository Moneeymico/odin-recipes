Spaghetti

This image shows an amazing plate of Spaghetti alla Sugo!

History

Noodles made of wheat and eggs were known in the Mediterranean world in antiquity, but the pasta we know as spaghetti—which takes its name from the Italian spaghetto, “little cord”—was probably introduced to Sicily by the island’s Arab conquerors in the 8th century

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tinned Tomatos
  • Nonnas Tomato Sauce

How to Cook

  1. Fry Onion and Garlic
  2. Add Tomato Paste
  3. Fry For 2-3mins
  4. Add Tinned Tomato
  5. Add Tomato Sauce
  6. Cook until thick
